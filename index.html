威氏国际WIZDII International, 总部设于英国伦敦，致力于推进英国、澳大利亚及其它英国联邦国家与中国本土学校的国际项目交流合作。威氏国际是受到英国文化教育委员会、澳大利亚新南威尔士州政府教育部官方认可和授权的教育机构。来自于英国以及澳大利亚的官方数据统计：威氏国际近5年已经成功为50多所中英、中澳学校结对国际友好学校，并在此框架下开展各项国际项目，包括：联合办学/授位、网络课程交互、课程植入、深度学术交流、联合课程开发、融合游学、师资培训交流、 夏令营等项目，共有3000+师生通过威氏国际这一官方认可渠道成功完成了国际交流项目。

WIZDII International, headquartered in central London, specializes in Sino-UK, Sino-Australia educational projects and other international programs between academic institutions in China and the British Commonwealth. WIZDII International is officially accredited by the British Council and officially authorized by Department of Education of New South Wales Government of Australia. According to the official statistics from the UK and Australia, in re cent 5 years, WIZDII International has succeeded in establishment of friend-school relations for over 50 Chinese and foreign academic institutions. And within the framework, various international programs have been carried out smooth, including joint school/diploma, online teaching, course inception, profound academic exchange, joint development of courses, integration study tour, teaching staff training, summer camp and etc. Meanwhile over 3,000 students and teaching staffs have been benefited from these international programs.
